Terrain Path

Choose Walking, Running, or Jumping on this sheet before moving the token. Tagged Regions record terrain at the token's native elevation, and whole-number elevation changes record level MP automatically. Native Walls stop movement and block weapon line of sight according to their Foundry settings. These fields remain available for corrections and manual facing costs. Clear, paved, and road hexes add no terrain cost. Jumping ignores terrain, level, and facing costs.

Physical Attacks

During the Physical Attack Phase, target one adjacent BattleMech. A confirmation window previews the complete target number, damage, limb restrictions, and hit-location table before rolling.

Kicks automatically resolve the target's Piloting Skill Roll on a hit or the attacker's roll after a miss, including falls, facing, grouped damage, and pilot injury checks.
